Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to *.m-sound.dev · Based in Switzerland

Who we are

M Sound Solutions is operated from Switzerland. If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, just reach out at info@m-sound.dev — we're happy to help.

What data we collect

We keep things minimal. When you visit this website, our hosting infrastructure (Cloudflare) automatically logs basic technical data — your IP address, the page you requested, your browser type, and the time of access. This is standard for any website and is used solely to keep things running smoothly and securely. We do not run any analytics, advertising trackers, or user profiling.

DNS via Cloudflare

This site is delivered via Cloudflare's global network. Cloudflare may process visitor data (including IP addresses) to protect against attacks and ensure reliability. They act as our data processor under a Data Processing Agreement. We are hosting this website on our own servers. We dont collect any informations who visites our site.

YouTube videos

Our homepage includes an embedded YouTube video. To protect your privacy, the video only loads after you give your consent via our cookie banner. We use YouTube's privacy-enhanced mode (youtube-nocookie.com), which means no cookies are set until you actually play the video. Once you do, Google/YouTube may collect data according to their own policy: policies.google.com/privacy
